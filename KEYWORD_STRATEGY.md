## üéØ Keyword Implementation Strategy

### Home Page
**Primary Focus:** pineapple farm Ghana, premium pineapples Ghana, sustainable farming Ghana

**Implementation:**
- H1: "Premium Pineapples from Ghana"
- Meta title: "Mennai Farms - Premium Pineapples from Ghana | Sustainable Farming"
- First paragraph: Include primary keywords naturally
- Alt text: Use keywords in image descriptions

### Products Page
**Primary Focus:** MD2 pineapple, Smooth Cayenne, Sugarloaf, wholesale pineapples

**Implementation:**
- H1: "Our Premium Pineapple Varieties"
- Product names as H2s with variety keywords
- Descriptions include variety-specific keywords
- Benefits and uses sections

### About Page
**Primary Focus:** sustainable farming, company history, Ghana agricultural business

**Implementation:**
- H1: "About Mennai Farms"
- Story section with sustainability keywords
- Mission/vision with farming practice keywords
- Location mentions (Marfokrom, Ayensuano)

### Farm Page
**Primary Focus:** pineapple plantation, farm operations, Marfokrom, Ayensuano District

**Implementation:**
- H1: "Our Pineapple Plantation"
- Location section with geographic keywords
- Farming methods with technique keywords
- Infrastructure and scale information

### Gallery Page
**Primary Focus:** farm photos, visual tour, pineapple plantation images

**Implementation:**
- H1: "Farm Photo Gallery"
- Image alt text with descriptive keywords
- Captions with location and activity keywords
- Categories for different farm aspects

### Contact Page
**Primary Focus:** contact pineapple farm, wholesale inquiry, export inquiry

**Implementation:**
- H1: "Contact Mennai Farms"
- Contact reasons with business keywords
- Location information with geographic keywords
- Call-to-action with intent keywords

## üìä Keyword Density Guidelines

**Optimal Keyword Density:** 1-2% of total content

**Best Practices:**
- Use primary keyword in H1 (once)
- Use in first 100 words of content
- Use in meta description
- Use in URL slug
- Include in image alt text
- Use naturally throughout content
- Don't over-optimize (keyword stuffing)
